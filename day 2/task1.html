Angela's Recipe for the best Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 3/4 cup warm milk
  • 21/2 teaspoons yeast
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

For the filling:

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 11/2 Tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 Cup butter
Instructions
  1. Mix the milk wuth the yeast,sugars,eggs
  2. Melt the butter abd add to the mixture
  3. Add in the flour and mix until combined into a dough.
  4. knead the dough for 10 min
  5. Transfer the dough into a large bowl and cover with plastic wrap.
